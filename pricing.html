Common questions

Is RangeIQ a gambling product?

No. RangeIQ is a strategy training and decision-support tool. No real money is wagered, no card games are played, and no betting is facilitated. It's an educational SaaS product, similar to chess training software or sports analytics platforms.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor. Subscriptions are billed in USD.

Do you offer refunds?

All sales are final. You can cancel anytime from your account settings to stop future charges — no refunds are issued for past billing periods. Because every paid user gets a free trial period before being charged, you have plenty of time to evaluate RangeIQ at no cost. See our Terms of Service for full details.

What happens after the free trial period ends on June 1?

If you sign up before June 1, you'll have full access during the launch period at no charge. Your subscription begins billing on June 1, 2026 at the rate you selected. You can cancel before then if you change your mind.
