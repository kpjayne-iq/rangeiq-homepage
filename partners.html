Common questions

What's the difference between the Partner Program and Premium?

The Partner Program is open to everyone and offers 25% recurring commission on every paid customer you refer — no application required. Premium is invitation-only for established creators with existing audiences, offering higher rates (up to 50%) and additional support. Most partners start with the open program.

Do I need approval to join?

No. The Partner Program is open — you can sign up instantly through the Tolt portal and get your tracking link within minutes.

When and how do I get paid?

Payouts are issued NET-15 — 15 days after the end of each month — via PayPal. Minimum payout is $10.

Is this really recurring revenue?

Yes. Every month a customer you referred stays subscribed, you earn your commission on that month's payment. If they stay 12 months at $14.99/month, that's $44.97 to you from a single referral.

How long is the tracking cookie?

90 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 90 days — even if they don't sign up right away — you still get credit. Most programs use 30 days; poker training is a considered purchase, so we use 90.

What if a customer cancels or refunds?

Commissions are tied to actual paid revenue. If a customer cancels, future commissions stop. If a refund is issued, the corresponding commission is reversed. This keeps incentives aligned — bring in real customers who stay.

Can I be both a Pro user and a Partner?

Yes — encouraged. The best partners are people who actually use the product. Self-referrals (signing up via your own link) are blocked, but using RangeIQ yourself and promoting it to your audience is exactly the model we want.

Are there content guidelines?

Yes. Promote RangeIQ honestly: as a strategy training and decision-support tool, not a gambling product. No earnings claims, no outcome promises, no promotion to minors. Full guidelines provided after sign-up.

Can I upgrade to Premium later?

Yes. High-performing partners may be invited to Premium once they've demonstrated consistent, quality referrals. You can also apply for Premium directly if you're an established creator.
