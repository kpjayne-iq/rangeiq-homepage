Common questions

What's the difference between Standard and Premium?

Commission rate and relationship. Standard is 25% recurring, self-serve, no application. Premium is 50% recurring, requires an application, and includes direct contact with the founder for custom deals or co-marketing.

How does Premium approval work?

Applications are reviewed manually. Most receive a response within 48 hours. We approve creators whose audiences align with RangeIQ's player base — serious live and online cash players studying to improve.

Do Premium Partners get promo codes for their audience?

Yes. Premium Partners get a custom promo code (e.g., your name or handle) that their audience can redeem at checkout. This does two things: it gives your audience a tangible incentive to sign up through you — typically a discount on their first month — and it ensures you get credit for referrals even when viewers don't click a tracking link. The specific discount and code format are set during Premium onboarding.

Are marketing assets available?

A marketing kit with logos, screenshots, and suggested copy is being built based on partner requests. Approved partners will be notified as assets become available in the Tolt dashboard.

How does referral tracking work?

Two ways. First, every partner gets a unique tracking link. When someone clicks that link, a 90-day cookie is set in their browser — meaning you get credit for any subscription they start within 90 days, even if they don't sign up immediately. Second (Premium only), a custom promo code gives you credit even when the viewer types it at checkout without clicking a link. This dual-attribution model is important for video and podcast creators whose audiences often don't click in-content links.

Why a 90-day cookie?

Most affiliate programs use 30-day cookies. RangeIQ uses 90 because poker training is a considered purchase — a viewer might watch your content, think about it for a month, and come back to sign up. 90 days makes sure you still get credit for that eventual conversion.

Is the commission really recurring?

Yes. Every month a customer you referred stays subscribed, you earn your commission rate on that month's payment. If they stay 12 months on the $14.99 plan, that's $44.97 to a Standard Partner or $89.94 to a Premium Partner from a single referral.

When and how do I get paid?

Payouts are issued NET-15 — 15 days after the end of each month. Minimum payout is $10. Payments are made via PayPal through our affiliate platform, Tolt.

What if a customer cancels or refunds?

Commissions are tied to actual paid revenue. If a customer cancels, future commissions stop. If a refund is issued, the corresponding commission is reversed. This keeps incentives aligned: bring in real customers who stay.

Are there content guidelines?

Yes. Promote RangeIQ honestly: as a strategy training and decision-support tool, not a gambling product. Don't make earnings claims, don't promise outcomes, don't promote to minors. Full guidelines provided after sign-up.

Can I be both a Pro user and a Partner?

Yes — encouraged, even. The best Partners are people who actually use the product. Self-referrals (signing up via your own link) are blocked, but using RangeIQ yourself and promoting it to your audience is exactly the model we want.

Is there an exclusivity requirement?

No. Promote whatever else you want. We just ask that when you talk about RangeIQ, you talk about it accurately.
