Who qualifies

Premium is designed for creators whose audiences align with RangeIQ's player base — serious live and online cash players studying to improve their game. We approve partners whose reach and credibility translate into quality referrals, not just volume.

Good fits typically include:

  • YouTube creators with established poker audiences (any size, if engaged)
  • Twitch streamers focused on cash games or strategy content
  • Podcasters covering poker strategy, theory, or the live circuit
  • Writers, coaches, and educators with newsletter subscribers or student bases
  • Working professionals in the poker industry (coaches, commentators, authors)

Not ready for Premium? The open Partner Program offers 25% recurring commission with no approval needed — a strong starting point for any creator building an audience.
