Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how RangeIQ Poker ("RangeIQ", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by IQ Digital Holdings LLC, a Nevada limited liability company, collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our marketing website (rangeiqpoker.com) and web application (app.rangeiqpoker.com), collectively the "Service".

By creating an account or otherwise using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy, and to being transparent about what we collect and why.

Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (required)
  • An encrypted password hash (we never store your plaintext password)
  • If you sign in with Google: your name, email address, and Google account identifier (we do not receive your Google password)
  • Your account creation date and last sign-in timestamp

Subscription and billing information. If you subscribe to a paid plan:

  • Subscription status, plan type, billing period, and renewal dates
  • Payment method metadata (card type, last four digits, expiration) — we do not store full card numbers; these are handled by our payment processor
  • Billing address and country (collected by our payment processor for tax compliance)
  • Transaction history including dates, amounts, currencies, and applicable taxes

Promotional grant information. If you redeem a comp or promotional access code:

  • The redemption code used, the date of redemption, and the duration of access granted
  • Notes associated with the grant (such as the source of the promotion)

Usage data. When you use the Service:

  • Pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, and session duration
  • Hand histories, saved spots, drill responses, and Study Vault entries you create within the app
  • Affiliate referral identifiers, if you arrived via an affiliate link
  • Error logs and crash reports (including browser console output) used to diagnose issues

Device and connection information. Automatically collected when you visit the Service:

  • IP address and approximate location (derived from IP, typically at the city level)
  • Browser type and version, operating system, and device type
  • Referring URL and the time and date of your visit

Communications. Any messages, support requests, or feedback you send to us, including the content of those messages and your contact information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide, operate, secure, and improve the Service
  • Generate IQ Reasoning explanations for engine recommendations using the limited hand-state data described in the Sub-Processors section
  • Authenticate you and maintain your active session
  • Process subscription payments, manage billing, and apply applicable taxes
  • Apply, validate, and enforce promotional grants and comp codes
  • Send transactional emails such as receipts, password resets, magic-link sign-in codes, comp expiration warnings, and account notifications
  • Send marketing communications (only if you have explicitly opted in by subscribing to our newsletter; you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Respond to your support inquiries and feedback
  • Track affiliate referrals and pay affiliate commissions
  • Detect, prevent, investigate, and address fraud, security incidents, and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and enforce our Terms of Service

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies. We do not use cookies for cross-site behavioral advertising.

Essential cookies. Required for the Service to function. These include:

  • Authentication cookies set by Supabase, which keep you signed in across pages and sessions
  • Session and security cookies used to prevent cross-site request forgery and similar attacks

Analytics. We use Vercel Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns. Vercel Analytics is designed to be privacy-friendly and does not use cookies for cross-site tracking.

Affiliate attribution. If you arrive at the Service via an affiliate link, our affiliate partner Tolt sets a first-party cookie (typical duration: 90 days) to attribute any subsequent subscription to that affiliate. Disabling this cookie has no effect on your ability to use the Service; it only affects affiliate attribution.

You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you disable essential cookies, parts of the Service may stop working, including your ability to remain signed in.

We currently do not display a cookie consent banner. If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom and wish to withdraw consent for non-essential cookies, you may do so by clearing cookies in your browser, by configuring your browser to block them, or by contacting us at privacy@rangeiqpoker.com.

Third-Party Services and Sub-Processors

We use the following third-party service providers ("sub-processors") to operate the Service. Each provider has its own privacy practices; we encourage you to review them:

Supabase
Authentication, user profile storage, subscription state, and redemption code records. Hosted in the United States. Privacy policy
Paddle.com Market Limited
Merchant of Record for all subscription billing. Paddle collects and processes payment information, calculates and remits applicable sales taxes, and handles invoicing. We do not receive or store your full card number. Privacy policy
Tolt
Affiliate referral tracking and commission management. Tolt receives transaction metadata to attribute affiliate referrals. Privacy policy
Resend
Transactional email delivery, including password resets, magic-link sign-in codes, and account notifications. Privacy policy
Google (Sign in with Google)
Optional federated sign-in. If you choose to sign in with Google, Google provides us with your name, email address, and Google account identifier. Privacy policy
Anthropic
Powers the IQ Reasoning explanation layer. When you request a recommendation, the engine sends the resulting hand state and recommended action (cards, board, position, archetype, sizing, action) to Anthropic to generate a coaching explanation. We do not send your account email, name, payment information, or any persistent user identifier with these requests. Anthropic does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models. Privacy policy
Vercel
Application hosting, content delivery, and privacy-friendly analytics. Privacy policy
Buttondown
Email newsletter management for users who opt in. Privacy policy
Google Fonts
Web font delivery for typography on rangeiqpoker.com.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Sub-processors are bound by data-processing agreements that limit their use of your information to the purposes for which we engage them.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With sub-processors as described in the previous section, under contractual confidentiality and data-protection obligations
  • With affiliates who referred your subscription, limited to commission-attribution metadata (we do not share your full account details with affiliates)
  • To comply with legal obligations, valid court orders, subpoenas, or government requests, or to defend against legal claims
  • To protect rights and safety, including investigating suspected fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service, and to protect the safety of our users or the public
  • In connection with a corporate transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets. In such an event, we will provide notice to affected users by email at least 30 days in advance of any change in how their information is controlled, where reasonably practicable

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS / TLS) for all communications between your device and the Service
  • Encryption at rest for stored data within Supabase
  • Hashed and salted password storage; we never store plaintext passwords
  • Restricted internal access to user data on a need-to-know basis
  • Payment card data handled exclusively by Paddle, a PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor; full card numbers never reach our servers

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law:

  • Active accounts: retained until you request deletion
  • Deleted accounts: personal information is purged from our active systems within 90 days of the deletion request, except where retention is required by law (such as billing records) or where information has been anonymized
  • Billing and transaction records: retained for at least 7 years to comply with applicable tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements
  • Support communications: retained for up to 3 years from the date of the last communication, then deleted
  • Server logs and analytics: retained for up to 12 months in identifiable form, then aggregated or deleted
  • Affiliate attribution cookies: retained for 90 days from initial visit, after which attribution expires

  • Right to know: request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing: we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. There is nothing to opt out of
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA/CPRA rights
  • Right to limit use of sensitive personal information: we do not use sensitive personal information for purposes that would trigger this right

To exercise any CCPA/CPRA right, contact us at privacy@rangeiqpoker.com. You may also designate an authorized agent to act on your behalf, subject to verification.
